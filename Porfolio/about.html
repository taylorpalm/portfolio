Hey there!

I'm Taylor Palm, a registered dietitian turned software developer. No you're right, they have nothing to do with eachother. I got my bachelor's in nutrition, master's in nutrition, became an RD and when it was all said and done, I realized I wanted to be a computer programmer.

I love puzzles. I've been known to sit at a puzzle for hours until I can barely see straight. I like to break things into pieces, figure out how they fit and that's where I fell in love with computer science. I went back to school shortly thereafter to complete a post-bacc in computer science at Stockton University and now I'm a few short months from graduation, eager to get into the field and start solving the world's biggest problems with code.

Aside from geeking out on computer things, my other hobbies include working out, reading restaurant menus, and buying plane tickets on a whim. I've been to 17 countries and counting(that pic to the left is Thailand), eaten all the weird foods abroad- bugs, chicken intestines, cow penis- you name it. I've ziplined in Costa Rica, drank steins at Oktoberfest, snorkled the Great Barrier Reef and loved every second. Well, that's enough about me for an "About Me."
